Resources for Finding Scholarships

Here are some key resources you will need to find scholarships:

  • Finaid.com – A large database of scholarships worldwide.
  • FastWeb – A platform for finding scholarships tailored to your profile.
  • ScholarshipPortal – A resource for international scholarships.
  • StudyAbroad – Resources for scholarships aimed at studying abroad.
